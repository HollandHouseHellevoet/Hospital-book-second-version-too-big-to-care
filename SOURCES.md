## File Naming Convention

When saving source material to the repo:

```
/source-material/
├── substack/
│   ├── five-families.md
│   ├── five-dynasties.md
│   └── [article-slug].md
├── data/
│   ├── nonprofit-charity-care.csv
│   ├── lobbying-spend-aha.csv
│   └── [dataset-name].csv
├── filings/
│   ├── 990-[system-name]-[year].pdf
│   └── [filing-type]-[entity]-[date].pdf
└── research/
    ├── jama-2023-charity-care.pdf
    └── [source]-[year]-[topic].pdf
```
