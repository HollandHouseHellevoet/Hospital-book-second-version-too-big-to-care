# Chapter 10: The Revolving Door

> *Staffers become lobbyists. Lobbyists become staffers. The door never stops spinning.*

---

## The Thesis

The people who write healthcare policy and the people who lobby on healthcare policy are the same people — just at different points in their careers. Chip Kahn went from Ways & Means to FAH. Dozens of others followed the same path. CMS administrators become industry consultants. Industry executives become regulators. The expertise excuse covers the capture reality.

---

## Section 1: The Pattern

- Congressional staffer → lobbying firm → back to government → back to lobbying
- CMS/HHS official → healthcare company → advisory role
- Trade association executive → government appointment → trade association
- The same 500 people rotating through the system
