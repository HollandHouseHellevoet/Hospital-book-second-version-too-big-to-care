# Chapter 8: The Committees

> *Four committees. Four gatekeepers. Every reform dies here.*

---

## The Thesis

Healthcare policy flows through four congressional committees. Senate Finance. Senate HELP. House Ways & Means. House Energy & Commerce. The members who sit on these committees receive the most healthcare money. The reforms that would help patients die in these committees. This is where the cartel's investment pays off.
