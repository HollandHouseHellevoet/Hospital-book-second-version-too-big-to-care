# Chapter 11: The Cracks

> *The castle has cracks. Some are widening.*

---

## The Thesis

The system that seemed invincible is showing stress fractures. UnitedHealth's public backlash. Price transparency pressure. The physician independence movement. Employers bypassing the system entirely. State-level reforms breaking through where federal reform fails. The cracks are real. The question is whether they'll widen or be patched.

---

## Section 1: The UnitedHealth Moment

- Brian Thompson shooting (December 2024)
- Public reaction: sympathy for the shooter
- "Deny, Delay, Depose" as rallying cry
- Congressional hearings, executive scrutiny
- The mask slipping on payer practices
