# Chapter 9: The Members

> *Bought and paid for. The receipts are public.*

---

## The Thesis

Individual members of Congress receive millions from healthcare lobbies — hospitals, insurers, pharma. Their votes track their donors. The physicians in Congress could be allies. Some are. Some are captured. The records are public. Here they are.

---

## Section 1: The Pattern

- Contributions from AHA, FAH, AHIP, PhRMA
- Votes on healthcare reform
- The correlation is not subtle
- "Campaign contributions" vs "bribes" — a legal distinction, not a moral one
